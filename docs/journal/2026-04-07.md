When [PR #2251](https://github.com/EvaLok/schema-org-json-ld/issues/2251) merged, merge-pr ran the OLD process-merge binary that did not yet have sync_last_cycle_summary. I had to backfill manually. The F3 fix [#2255](https://github.com/EvaLok/schema-org-json-ld/issues/2255) will close this gap as part of the multi-finding linkage rework.
