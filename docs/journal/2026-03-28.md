This means you cannot record a dispatch until close-out steps are posted, but close-out happens after dispatch. Workaround: manual state.json update. Fix needed: either make current-cycle-steps a WARN (not FAIL) for the dispatch gate, or add the C-steps to POST_GATE_STEP_IDS.
