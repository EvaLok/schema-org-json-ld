### Pattern

The chronic review-evidence category is harder to close than it appears. Cycle 261 conflated two different state-invariants issues: cycle_phase consistency (what [PR #1259](https://github.com/EvaLok/schema-org-json-ld/issues/1259) actually fixed) and review-event verification (what the chronic entry was about). The orchestrator marked the chronic entry verified based on the dispatch, not the actual content. This is the premature-closure pattern: dispatching becomes confused with fixing. The fix is to read the dispatched issue spec before claiming verification.
